Long ago, in the kingdom of Logan surrounded by coal mines and rednecks,
legends told of an omnipotent and omniscient power which resided in a young programmer named Joe.
This power, which resided within Joe’s heart,
would grant the deepest desires in the heart and mind of the person touching it.
Many people aggressively sought to take this power,
but no one ever returned...
One day a former acquaintance of the programmer contacted him.
It seemed that Princess Shelly, exhausted from the seemingly endless line of moronic assholes,
was seeking a suitor with a brain.
After vanquishing the enemies within the Kingdom of Logan,
Joe traveled to meet up with the princess in the far off kingdom town of Cross Lanes.
From there the two fought through the dilapidated kingdoms of Huntington and Gallipolis
and arrived in the golden land of Columbus.
It was here that Joe decided to finally share his power…

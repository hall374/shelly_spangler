Her awards and accolades include the coveted Most-Awesomest Cooking Award from Tim Hall, the super-coveted Kick-Ass Mother Award from Dr. Stephen Peyton, Ph.D, the ultra-coveted Best Combination Since Guns N' Roses Award from Jack Daniel's for her Internationally-famous Jack & Coke recipe, and the mega-astronomically-colossally-tremendously-epically-envy-inducingly-really really wantingly-coveted Most Beautiful, Sexy Creature to Ever Walk the Earth (past, present, and future) Award from the real-life Superman, Joe Spangler.
